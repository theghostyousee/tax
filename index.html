If you make the biggest buy (in tokens) you become the TaxCollector, and collect 3% fees (in AVAX) the same way marketing does.
If you sell any tokens at all at any point you can never be the TaxCollector.
If someone beats your record, they become the TaxCollector.
If you sell while the TaxCollector, the marketing wallet becomes TaxCollector and the record is reset to 0, this way no one can buy a ton and then sell and keep collecting tax fees.
The TaxCollector is set via biggest buy in tokens.
The Max Wallet starts low and increases +.0.1% with every transaction, so over time the buys will scale and it will cost more and more AVAX to beat the record.
The point is to reward big buys.
There are no punishments other than disallowing sellers to be the TaxCollector.
